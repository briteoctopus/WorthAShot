      Other Measurements

      Splash

      A small amount generally used to add color or a hint of flavor to a drink.

      Dash

      Smaller than a splash. There is no formal measurement, but generally one shake of a bitter bottle, or 5-6 drops.

      Float

      A layer. It is a small amount that goes on top of ("floats on") the mixed drink.

      Part

      A "part" can be any amount you want. It all depends on how big you want your drink to be. (Use the same amount for each part. Ex. 1 part is 1 cup, 2 parts is 2 cups, etc.)
